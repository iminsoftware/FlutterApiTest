## 💡 Common Use Cases

### POS System
- **Display**: Show customer-facing information
- **Cashbox**: Open cash drawer after payment
- **Scanner**: Scan product barcodes
- **NFC**: Accept contactless payments

### Retail
- **Scale**: Weigh products
- **Scanner**: Inventory management
- **RFID**: Asset tracking
- **Camera**: QR code scanning

### Restaurant
- **Display**: Show order details to customer
- **Serial**: Connect to kitchen printer
- **Light**: Order status indicators
